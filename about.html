Viva Chess Club is a welcoming community for chess enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. The club provides a friendly environment where members can learn, practice, and compete while developing strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. Regular activities include casual games, training sessions, tournaments, and coaching opportunities for beginners and advanced players alike. Viva Chess Club encourages sportsmanship, concentration, and teamwork, creating a space where players can share their passion for chess and grow both socially and intellectually.

The club is open on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays from 16:00 to 18:00, and on Sundays from 12:00 to 15:00.
